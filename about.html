About EY Trading

Enjoy Yourself Trading Inc. is focused on handcrafted fragrance, décor, and gift products created for homes, gifting, boutiques, and retail presentation.

Who We Are

EY Trading is built around decorative products that combine warmth, elegance, and handcrafted character. We believe beautiful presentation and thoughtful detail create lasting value for both gifting and everyday use.

What We Do

We produce and supply handmade candles, flameless reed diffusers, jewelry, and decorative gift products suitable for retail, seasonal collections, boutique display, and personal gifting.

Our Vision & Values

We aim to build a trusted decorative product brand known for style, giftability, and refined presentation.

Elegant Design

We focus on products that look polished, warm, and suitable for modern homes, gifting, and boutique retail environments.

Handcrafted Appeal

Our collections are designed to feel personal and thoughtful, with more character than generic mass-market products.

Gift-Ready Presentation

We value attractive presentation and product combinations that work well for gifting, occasions, and seasonal collections.

Business Cooperation

We welcome retail partnerships, wholesale inquiries, and custom opportunities for businesses and event-related needs.

Our Mission

To deliver decorative products that bring beauty, fragrance, warmth, and gifting value to homes, boutiques, special occasions, and curated collections.

Who We Serve

We serve individual buyers, gift shoppers, boutiques, market vendors, event planners, and retail partners looking for attractive handcrafted products with strong presentation value.
