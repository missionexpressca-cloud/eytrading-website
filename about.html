About EY Trading

Enjoy Yourself Trading Inc. is focused on handcrafted fragrance, décor, and gift products created for homes, gifting, boutiques, and elegant retail presentation.

Who We Are

EY Trading is a decorative product brand centered around warmth, elegance, and handcrafted appeal. We believe beautiful presentation, thoughtful design, and giftable products create lasting value for both personal enjoyment and meaningful occasions.

What We Do

We produce and supply handmade candles, flameless reed diffusers, jewelry, and decorative gift products suitable for boutique retail, gifting, seasonal collections, and refined home décor.

Our Brand Philosophy

We create collections that combine aesthetic appeal, fragrance, handcrafted detail, and strong presentation value.

Warm & Elegant Design

Our products are designed to feel soft, warm, and refined, suitable for modern homes, gifting, and stylish display.

Handcrafted Character

We value detail, texture, and visual charm, creating products that feel more personal than ordinary mass-market items.

Gift-Ready Collections

Our collections are well suited for birthdays, seasonal occasions, curated gift sets, and thoughtful decorative gifting.

Retail-Friendly Presentation

We focus on product categories that display beautifully in boutiques, market booths, pop-up shops, and decorative retail shelves.

Our Mission

To create decorative products that bring beauty, fragrance, warmth, and gifting value to homes, special occasions, boutiques, and curated collections.

Our Vision

To grow EY Trading into a trusted decorative brand known for elegant handcrafted products, giftable appeal, and thoughtful business cooperation.

Who We Serve

Our products are suitable for both individual buyers and business customers.

Gift Shoppers

Customers looking for decorative, meaningful, and beautifully presented gifts.

Boutiques & Gift Shops

Retail businesses seeking handcrafted decorative products with strong shelf appeal.

Event & Seasonal Buyers

Customers and organizers looking for occasion-based décor, gifting, and themed collections.

Wholesale & Business Partners

Businesses interested in product sourcing, curated collections, and long-term cooperation opportunities.

Why Work With Us

We combine handcrafted appeal, attractive presentation, and flexible cooperation opportunities to support both retail and gifting needs.

Looking Ahead

We continue to expand our decorative product collections with a focus on fragrance, warmth, gifting value, and elegant visual presentation.
