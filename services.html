Services

From international inbound parcels to local B2B runs, Mission Go provides structured last‑mile and regional delivery services.

Last‑mile parcel delivery

Doorstep delivery for standard parcels and 20kg+ packages, with clear time windows and status reporting back to your platform or carrier.

  • Same‑day / next‑day options by zone
  • Driver app with scan‑to‑deliver workflow
  • Photo / signature proof of delivery where required

Pickup point & counter services

We operate or partner with neighborhood pickup points so customers can collect parcels at convenient times and locations.

  • Parcel check‑in / check‑out with ID or code
  • Storage rules and time limits agreed in advance
  • Signage and basic customer support in English & Chinese

Line‑haul & local distribution

Our core service area covers Saskatchewan, including Saskatoon, Regina, Prince Albert, Moose Jaw, and surrounding communities, with scheduled B2B routes for local businesses.

  • Fixed routes with agreed departure / arrival windows
  • Option to combine with last‑mile delivery in each city
  • Flexible vehicle sizes depending on volume

China–Canada Consolidation Shipping

Ship your China online purchases to our China consolidation warehouse. We photo/weigh on arrival, optional repacking, then send via LCL sea freight to Canada. After customs clearance, we deliver from Moose Jaw.
